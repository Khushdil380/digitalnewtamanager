Here is My Key Idea about this project
As you may already know, in middle-class Indian weddings, First the marriage decision is finalized, then every guest get printed. invitation cards.  on each card we-write the guest's name and deliver them to the guests' homes prior to the wedding day. On the day of the wedding, guests arrive and eat food and refreshments. Additionally, a designated person sits with a register; as each guest arrives, they offer a monetary contribution and record their name and address in the register. some guest gives envelope, because at that moment they dont want to show how much they contribute 
In rural parlance, this custom is referred to as 'Newta System'. so i want to make "DigitalNewtaManager" website - To manage this invited people list and on the wedding day to track exactly who attended the personally and how much they contribute (gives Newta) and who send via other people and dont attended personally.

Core Goals of the System
The system should allow a user to:
1. Create an account and login
2. Manage multiple weddings/events
3. Maintain a list of invited guests
4. Track invitation distribution
5. Record guest attendance
6. Record money or gift contributions
7. Quickly search guests during the event
8. Export records
9. Switch UI color themes
